Rachel Levine

Professor in Liberal Arts and Humanities at Marianopolis College

I teach Humanities and Liberal Arts courses on the ancient and medieval world, including ancient art history, Roman social history, and classical literature in translation, with a particular emphasis on religion, art, and science. My current interests are the relationship between technology, education, and the ancient world.
